About Me

I am a Food Scientist with a Ph.D. in Food Science (Federal University of Rio de Janeiro, Brazil) and a degree from the Federal University of Mato Grosso (Brazil), specializing in Food Microbiology and Bioinformatics.
Currently, I am starting a new research position at the University of Lethbridge (Canada), funded by Mitacs and BCRC.
My main goal is to understand and develop strategies to prevent biofilm formation by Escherichia coli in slaughterhouses.

I am passionate about open science, reproducibility, and collaborative projects in food safety and microbial genomics.

Areas: Genomics • Food Microbiology • Data Science • Bioinformatics • Foodborne • Bacteria

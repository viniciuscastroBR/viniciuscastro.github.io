About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher in Food Microbiology and Bioinformatics at UFMT, Brazil.
My research focuses on bacterial genomics, antimicrobial resistance, and developing tools for biological data analysis.

I am passionate about open science, reproducibility, and collaborative projects in food safety and microbial genomics.

Areas: Genomics • Food Microbiology • Data Science • Python • Bash • Linux

Research

I work on the genomic characterization of bacterial isolates from food and environmental samples.
My interests include:

  • Antimicrobial resistance and virulence gene detection
  • Pangenome analysis and phylogeny
  • Microbiome studies in food ecosystems
  • Developing open-source pipelines and reproducible scripts for genomics

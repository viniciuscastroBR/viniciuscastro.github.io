Who I Am

I am a Food Scientist, graduated in 2015 from the Faculty of Nutrition (FANUT) at the Federal University of Mato Grosso (UFMT). I hold a Master's degree in Animal Science (2017), supervised by Prof. Eduardo Figueiredo. I completed my Ph.D. in Food Science in 2021, guided by Prof. Carlos Conte at the Chemistry Institute (IQ) of the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro (UFRJ). My Ph.D. included an exchange program at Agriculture and Agri-food Canada and the Government of Alberta, under the supervision of Dr. Kim Stanford and Dr. Tim McAllister. Additionally, I have 52 accepted and/or published scientific articles, authored 13 papers presented at national and international conferences, served as a reviewer for 20 scientific journals, including The Lancet Microbe and The Lancet Health - Europe, and am a member of the editorial panel for the Canadian Journal of Infectious Diseases and Medical Microbiology, Frontiers in Genetics, Frontiers in Microbiology, and Antibiotics
